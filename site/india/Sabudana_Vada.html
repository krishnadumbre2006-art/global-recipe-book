Crunchy sabudana vadas full of flavor, best enjoyed hot with spicy chutney!

Sabudana vada is a traditional deep-fried Indian snack, originating from Maharashtra, that is crunchy on the outside and soft on the inside. Made from tapioca pearls (sabudana), mashed potatoes, and crushed peanuts, it is a popular dish during Hindu fasting periods, such as Navratri, as well as a general tea-time or street snack.
