Poha Recipe

About Poha

Poha is a light, healthy, and delicious Indian breakfast dish made from flattened rice. It's quick to prepare and packed with flavor, often garnished with coriander, peanuts, and lemon juice. Perfect for busy mornings!

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups poha (flattened rice)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 medium potato, diced (optional)
  • ¼ cup peanuts
  • ½ tsp mustard seeds
  • 8–10 curry leaves
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and lemon juice for garnish

Steps to Make Poha

  1. Rinse the poha in a strainer under running water. Let it sit for 5 minutes to soften.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Once they splutter, add curry leaves, peanuts, and green chili.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent. Optionally, add diced potatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Stir in turmeric powder and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add the softened poha and mix gently on low flame for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Turn off the heat, garnish with coriander and a squeeze of lemon juice. Serve warm!

Serving Suggestion

Serve hot poha with a cup of chai or curd. You can also top it with sev or grated coconut for extra texture.

Pro Tips

  • Do not over-soak the poha; it should be soft but not mushy.
  • Add a pinch of sugar for a balanced flavor.
  • You can mix in veggies like peas or carrots for a healthier version.
  • Roast the peanuts separately for extra crunch.
